## AK-112 Assault Rifle Remix: AKS-112U (w/ modded variants)

### Info:
An AKS-74U or "Krinkov" type of modification of my Assault Rifle Remix. I cut most of the barrel off to give it a more compact look.

Made by **NovaRain**\
The drum magazine is from FO2: Oblivion Lost mod by **Rainman** and **Gumych**.

### Tools used:
* Titanium FRM Browser 1.3
* Frame Animator 2.70.1
* [Paint.NET](https://www.getpaint.net)
* [GIMP](https://www.gimp.org)
* [PNGOUT](http://advsys.net/ken/utils.htm)
